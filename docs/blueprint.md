# **App Name**: Neighboro Connect

## Core Features:

- Responsive Landing Page Layout: A mobile-first, single-page layout that adapts seamlessly to various screen sizes, ensuring accessibility and a clean user experience on any device.
- Dual App Information Display: Clear and distinct sections providing comprehensive information for both the 'Neighboro (Customer App)' and 'Neighboro Partner (Shop Owner App)'.
- Dynamic Content Sections: Dedicated sections for features, benefits, 'how-it-works', and 'why choose us' for both customer and shop owner perspectives.
- Google Play Store Download Buttons: Prominently featured, clickable Google Play Store badges/buttons for easy download of both mobile applications.
- Integrated Legal & Information Pages: Dedicated static pages for the Privacy Policy and Account Deletion process, ensuring legal compliance and user trust.
- SEO Metadata Management: Functionality to easily configure meta tags, descriptions, and keywords to optimize the landing page for search engines and app store visibility.
- AI Localized Content Tool: An AI tool to generate suggested copy variations for headlines, calls-to-action, and benefit statements, incorporating a local 'Hinglish feel' and relevant keywords to improve conversion rates and regional appeal.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep periwinkle blue (#2E2EB3) for a sense of professionalism and trustworthiness, symbolizing technology and community connection.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue-grey (#F6F6FA) providing a clean and open canvas, visibly derived from the primary hue but much brighter.
- Accent color: A vibrant cyan-blue (#2AADD1) to highlight calls-to-action, important links, and interactive elements, offering a fresh, modern contrast to the primary.
- Supplementary accents: Use warm, inviting orange (#E55D0A) and vibrant green (#4CAF50) tones judiciously for iconography, success messages, or other brand visual cues to fulfill 'Indian-friendly' color requests and add warmth.
- Headlines: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, slightly techy, and bold feel, grabbing attention effectively.
- Body text: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its neutrality, excellent readability across all devices, and clean aesthetic, supporting a professional yet approachable tone.
- Utilize clean, modern, and friendly flat-style icons. Prefer linear or minimally-filled icon designs that align with the minimalist aesthetic. Incorporate primary, accent, and supplementary colors.
- Emphasize a mobile-first, grid-based layout with ample whitespace to ensure clarity, focus, and breathability, enhancing the 'clean' and 'minimalist' feel. Implement responsive imagery and content blocks.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful micro-animations and transitions on interactive elements like buttons and navigation items. Use gentle fades or slides for section reveals to create a smooth, modern user experience without being distracting.
